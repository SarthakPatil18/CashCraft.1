Build a premium fintech application called **CashCraft** that works seamlessly on both **mobile and desktop (responsive web app)**.

The experience should feel like a high-end product (Apple-level polish) with a black-and-white minimalist design and smooth animations.

---

### 🎯 Core Concept

CashCraft is a gamified finance app where users interact with:

* Present Self
* Disciplined Future Self
* Chaotic Future Self

Every financial action updates a **Future Score** and shows simulated consequences.

---

### 🎨 Design System

* Colors: Black (#000000), White (#FFFFFF), Greys
* Accent: subtle glow (white/blue)
* Style: minimal, glassmorphism, soft shadows
* Typography: modern (Inter / SF Pro style)
* Animations: smooth, premium, non-flashy

---

### 📱📺 Responsive Layout (VERY IMPORTANT)

#### Mobile:

* Bottom navigation bar
* Full-screen immersive experience
* Swipe interactions
* Focus on simplicity

#### Desktop:

* Sidebar navigation (left side)
* Multi-panel layout:

  * Left: Navigation
  * Center: Main content
  * Right: Insights / Future projections
* Hover effects + micro-interactions
* Keyboard-friendly navigation
* Wider dashboards with more data visibility

Ensure seamless responsiveness between screen sizes.
